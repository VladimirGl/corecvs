Detailed Description

template<typename LinearType>
class corecvs::Affine3D< LinearType >

Template class that holds the affine transformation of the space in form of rotational/shear part and shift part.

Generally it is either quaternion based (and can't hold shear/scale on this case) or Matrix33 based.
