Frames and coordinate systems

We have following coordinate systems:

  • World
  • Camera related
  • Image related
  • Rectified image related

coordinate system

This system is one of the widely used systems to describe 3D objects position in space. It is chosen based on convenience. For example this could be distances measured from the corner of the room.

related coordinate system

This system is related to camera. It's origin is in the camera's optical center

related coordinate system

This system is related to top left corner of the image

To transform Camera related to Image related systems you may use matrix K formed by function

corecvs::CameraIntrinsics::getKMatrix()
