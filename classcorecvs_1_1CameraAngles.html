Detailed Description

As rotation depends on the order of application of the rotations of Euler angles this class specifies one of possible orders that will be used for camera rotation description.

  • -to the right of the image (from top left to the top right corner)
  • -downwards (from top left to the bottom left corner)
  • -orthogonal, goes from the camera into the scene
